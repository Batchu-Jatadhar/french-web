Strasbourg is the capital of the Alsace region in northeastern France, located on the border with Germany. It is famous for its fairytale-like half-timbered houses, stunning Gothic cathedral, and a unique blend of French and German cultures. The city is also home to the European Parliament, making it an important political hub. With its rich history, scenic canals, and delicious Alsatian cuisine, Strasbourg is one of the most charming cities in France.
